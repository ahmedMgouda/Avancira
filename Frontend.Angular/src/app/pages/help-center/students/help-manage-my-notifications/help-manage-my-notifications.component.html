Manage my notifications

The management of your notifications is in the tab My account then in the section “Notifications”.

ℹ️ We advise you to leave your notifications active (in particular SMS) so as not to miss any exchange with your tutor.

SMS

  • Lesson requests: concerns the acceptance or refusal of your lesson requests as well as your exchanges with your tutor.

E-mail

  • Account Activity: Notification about your account, changes to it and your subscription.
  • Lesson requests: Notifications concerning the acceptance or refusal of your lesson requests as well as your exchanges with your tutors.
  • Offers concerning my ads: Notifications if you are also a tutor on the platform.
