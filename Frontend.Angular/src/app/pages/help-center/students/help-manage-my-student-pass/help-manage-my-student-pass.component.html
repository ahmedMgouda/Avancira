Manage my Student Pass

Manage your subscription from the Student Pass tab on your dashboard (expiration date, remaining days, etc.).

  • To suspend it, simply click on the “Suspend” button.
    The subscription will be suspended but you will keep all your benefits until there are no remaining days left 💛
  • To reactivate a suspended subscription, click on “Reactivate” and you're done.

ℹ️ If your Student Pass has ended and you wish to subscribe again, simply contact a new tutor!
