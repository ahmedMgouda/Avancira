Subscribe to the Student Pass to contact a tutor

After selecting a tutor and making your request, you must subscribe to the Student Pass.

The advantages of the Student Pass

  • A no-commitment subscription that allows you to contact all tutors for 30 days
  • The possibility to book and pay for your lessons 100% secure
  • And other advantages to discover in this article!

This subscription corresponds to the connection costs which contribute to the proper functioning of Avancira and covers the costs of our services, such as user assistance and dedicated tools for your courses.

ℹ️ The amount of the Student Pass is only debited if the tutor accepts your request.

You can subscribe by credit card or with your Paypal account, double authentication may be required.
