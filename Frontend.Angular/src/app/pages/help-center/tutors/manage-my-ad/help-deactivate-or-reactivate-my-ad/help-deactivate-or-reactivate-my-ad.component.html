Deactivate or reactivate my ad

Are you temporarily unavailable?

You can easily deactivate your ad at any time on your profile!

To deactivate your ad:

  • Click on the “My Tutor Ads” tab
  • Click on the “Ad online” button
  • A confirmation request will appear. Click on “Deactivate this ad” to confirm your choice.

To reactivate your ad:

  • Click on the “My Tutor Ads” tab
  • Click on the “Ad offline” button to put your ad back online.
