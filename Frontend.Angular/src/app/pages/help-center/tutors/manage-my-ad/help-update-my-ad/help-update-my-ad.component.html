Update my ad

Have there been changes in your availability, your location, or the topics you teach? Update your ad without delay! You can easily make changes to your ad via your profile.

  • Click on the “My Tutor Ads” tab
  • Click on the relevant ad and then on the section you wish to change
  • Make your changes and validate them ✅

Any changes you make take effect immediately, and your updated ad will be visible on Avancira straight away.

ℹ️ Tip: Preview your updated ad, so that you know what it will look like on the Avancira website. To do this, click on the “Student’s View” button.
