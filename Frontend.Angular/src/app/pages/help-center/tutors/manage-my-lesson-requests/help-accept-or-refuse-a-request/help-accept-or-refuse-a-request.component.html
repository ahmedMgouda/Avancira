Accept or refuse a request

Congratulations! A student has sent you a lesson request, and we have sent you an email and/or SMS to let you know.

Avancira will inform you by email and SMS. So remember to set your notifications. Make sure your notifications are set up correctly (Manage my notifications).

Accepting a request

Go to your messages and click on “Accept”.
You can then communicate directly with the student to schedule the first lesson.

Refusing a request

If you're not available, click on “Refuse” from your “My Messages” tab.
You can leave a personalized explanation for the student:

  • Click “Decline” twice
  • Then select “Other”
  • Write your message, then validate it!

ℹ️ Respond quickly, as this is highly appreciated by students!

If you’ve accepted or rejected a request in error, contact our team at hey@avancira.com.
