Leave or request a review

As a Avancira, it is very important that you receive reviews from your students!

Both you and your student are able to leave each other a review 7 days after you have accepted their lesson request.

To leave a review, click on the “Evaluate” button in your chat. Your student can do the same from their own page.

ℹ️ Send a gentle reminder asking your student to leave a review, as this will help optimize and boost your ad.
