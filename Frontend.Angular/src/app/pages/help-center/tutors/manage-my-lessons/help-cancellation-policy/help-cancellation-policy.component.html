Cancellation Policy

Have you scheduled a course on the platform? The student can cancel the lesson more than 24 hours before the lesson time and they will be fully refunded.

Beyond this period, your cancellation policy is taken into account and the course is refunded according to your level of compensation (at 0, 50, or 100%).

To add your cancellation policy:

  • Access the “My account” tab
  • “My Payments”
  • “Cancellation Policy”

You can indicate the desired level of compensation (from 0, 50, or 100%). The change will be automatically saved for your next lessons.

ℹ️ Inform your student before scheduling a lesson!
