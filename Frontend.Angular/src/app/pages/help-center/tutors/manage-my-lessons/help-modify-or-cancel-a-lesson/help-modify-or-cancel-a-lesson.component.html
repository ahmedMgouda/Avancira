Modify or cancel a lesson

Do you want to change the date of an already scheduled class? Go to your lesson page.

  • Click on the scheduled lesson (3 small dots will appear in the desktop version)
  • Click on modify the course or cancel the lesson
  • For a modification, choose the new date on which you would like the lesson to take place

If you cancel the lesson, your student will receive a notification and be refunded. For a modification, if your student is not available on the new dates, the original date of the lesson will be maintained.
