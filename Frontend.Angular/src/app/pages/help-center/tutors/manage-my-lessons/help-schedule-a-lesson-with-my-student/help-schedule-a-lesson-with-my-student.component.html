Schedule a lesson with my student

Avancira allows you to schedule and charge for your online class hours.

To send a lesson proposal to your student:

  • Go to your “My Messages”
  • Click “Propose a lesson”
  • Offer up to three availabilities (the student will select only one if available)
  • Choose the date, time and the lesson location
  • Then confirm the lesson

Your student will receive a notification. If he is not available, he can offer you new dates. It will then be your turn to accept his proposal. You and your student can also refuse the proposals if you cannot find an arrangement.
