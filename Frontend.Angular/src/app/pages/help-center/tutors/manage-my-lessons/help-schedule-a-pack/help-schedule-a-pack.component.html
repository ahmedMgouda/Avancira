Schedule a pack

With Avancira, you have the possibility of offering several courses at once, at a generally more advantageous price. These are called lesson packs. They can be 5 hours or 10 hours of lessons. You can determine their price in the “Rates” section of the ad.

To send a pack proposal to your student:

  • Go to your “My Messages”
  • Click on “Propose a pack” at the bottom right
  • Choose the desired pack (5 hours or 10 hours)
  • Check the total price of the pack
  • Then confirm the pack

If your student accepts, you receive 50% of the payment immediately after validation.

To receive the second part, remember to schedule all the lesson hours (5 hours or 10 hours) with your student using the schedule a lesson button. No additional payment will be requested.

ℹ️ Are you an Ambassador? The full payment will be transferred upon validation of the student!
