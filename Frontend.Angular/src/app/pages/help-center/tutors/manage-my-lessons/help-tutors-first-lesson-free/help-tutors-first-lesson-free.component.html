1st lesson free

The first session is ideal for getting to know your student, understanding their aims and objectives, and agreeing on a teaching plan that works for everyone.

You can choose the length of the free session from the following: 30 minutes, 45 minutes or 1 hour.

All tutors who do not yet have “Super Tutor” status are required to offer a free upfront session, and the “1st lesson free” tag will automatically appear on their ads.

After becoming a Super Tutor, you can decide whether or not to continue to offer students a free lesson.

ℹ️ Tutors who offer their first lesson free are contacted 10 times more than those who don’t!
