Report a lesson

Did the lesson not take place or did a problem arise? You can report the lesson from your chat:

  • Click on the lesson in question
  • Click on the 3 small dots that appear “...”
  • Click “Report a problem”
  • Indicate the reason for the report

ℹ️ You can report a class within 24 hours of the lesson taking place.

Your report will be sent to our teams who will get back to you.
