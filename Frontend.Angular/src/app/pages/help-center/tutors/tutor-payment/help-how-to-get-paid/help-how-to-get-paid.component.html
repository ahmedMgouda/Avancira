How to get paid?

For the payment of your lessons, you have two options:

  • Off-platform payment
  • Online payment

Off-platform payment

For this option, your only contact is your student. Together, choose the payment method that suits you best.

Online payment

Avancira provides you with a fully secure payment platform!

ℹ If you do not benefit from the Premium advantage, a commission of 10% will be applied.

The amount will be visible on your balance 24 hours after the lesson is taken. To access your balance:

  • “My account” tab
  • “My payments”
  • “Payment history”

Your balance will be transferred to your bank or Paypal account within 3 working days if the amount is at least $30. Opting for online payment on Avancira offers many advantages:

  • Securing your payments
  • Payment at the end of the course or monthly
  • Zero commission with the Premium subscription
  • Dedicated team to support you!
