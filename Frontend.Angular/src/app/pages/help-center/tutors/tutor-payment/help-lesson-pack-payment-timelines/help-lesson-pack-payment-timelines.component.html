Lesson / pack payment timelines

Have you scheduled a lesson or a pack on the platform? Here are the deadlines:

Payment timeframe for your lessons

  • 24 hours after the lesson date: you will receive the amount in your balance
  • 3 working days after the lesson date: you will receive the amount in your Paypal account

Payment timeframe for your packs

As soon as the pack is validated, your student is debited the full payment. The first part of the payment (50%) is visible in your balance and transferred to your Paypal account within 3 working days.

To receive the second part, schedule all the hours of the pack via the platform.

ℹ️ If you are an Ambassador, payment is visible on your balance as soon as the pack is validated, then within 3 working days on your bank or Paypal account.
