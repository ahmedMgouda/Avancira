Payment Receipts

This article is about payments made through the platform. For each payment received, find your payment receipts in your account.

To access it:

  • Go to your dashboard
  • Click on the “My Account” tab
  • Click on my payments “My payments”

You can then consult your “Payment history”. Under your Avancira balance, you will find the history of all the lessons or packs programmed with your students.

To select a specific payment, click on the “Payment from...” button and your payment receipt will appear!
